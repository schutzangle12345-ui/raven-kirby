The Story

Why we founded Raven Kirby

In the explosion of the AI era, we noticed a recurring pattern. Founders were building incredible prototypes, but they were hitting a wall when it came to enterprise-grade delivery, pricing models, and data strategy.

Generic startup playbooks treat AI like any other SaaS. They ignore the infrastructure costs, the data compliance hurdles, and the nuance of usage-based pricing in 2025. We realized that AI founders didn't need more "strategy decks"—they needed a partner who understands the stack as well as the sales motion.

We launched Raven Kirby to bridge the gap between AI technical nuance and Go-To-Market execution. Our approach combines fractional advisory with tactical delivery. We don't just tell you to "scale"; we build the playbooks that make scaling possible without the founder being the bottleneck for every single delivery.

Today, we serve solo founders, CTOs of seed-stage companies, and venture studios who need to de-risk their AI portfolios fast. We help you choose the right LLM stack, define your ICP, and experiment with pricing tiers that actually turn a profit.

Our Values
